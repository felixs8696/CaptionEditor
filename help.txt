[shiftIndex] - shifts specified ranges of subtitles indices by an amount inputted by the user
[shiftTime] - shifts a specified range of subtitles times by an time amount inputted by the user
[shiftTimeByIndex] - shifts times of subtitles by an inputted amount within a specified range of indicies
[IndexGen] - Generates indices for all of your subtitles (if they don't already exist)
[quit] - quits the program
